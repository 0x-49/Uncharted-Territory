No. Uncharted Territory is education-first. TSDR and Bracco show their full process — not just alerts. You get nightly workshops, daily Zooms, and post-market reviews. The goal is to teach you to trade independently.

Primarily US stocks, ETFs, and indices — with a heavy focus on QQQ, IWM, and sector ETFs. TSDR specializes in swing trades on stocks and indices. Bracco is a day trader. Options, crude oil, and futures analysis is also covered regularly.
Can beginners join?
Yes. Bracco and TSDR are described by members as "patient and willing teachers." The daily Zoom, nightly workshops, and active Q&A make the community accessible to traders at all levels — from day-one beginners to 8-year veterans.
